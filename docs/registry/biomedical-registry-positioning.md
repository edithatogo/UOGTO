# Biomedical Registry Positioning Decision

Track: `uogto_biomedical_registry_positioning_20260721`
GitHub issue: <https://github.com/edithatogo/UOGTO/issues/94>

## Decision

UOGTO should remain a conditional, evidence-backed candidate for BioPortal and OBO Foundry rather than being submitted immediately. UOGTO has defensible health-related applications, as documented in the OLS response, but its shared ontology is intentionally a general game-theoretic semantics ontology rather than a biomedical ontology.

## Evidence and disposition

| Target | Current fit | Decision | Trigger for reconsideration |
|---|---|---|---|
| BioPortal | Biomedical portal; UOGTO has health applications but no biomedical-only scope | Defer | An approved biomedical positioning note, target ontology scope, metadata package, and a concrete biomedical use case |
| OBO Foundry | Governance ecosystem for biological and biomedical ontologies | Defer / not prioritized | A demonstrated biological or biomedical ontology governance fit and alignment with OBO Foundry expectations |

## Reusable positioning

UOGTO can support game-theoretic modelling in genomics, clinical genetics, paediatrics, health economics, public-health and behavioural interaction models, mechanism design, and health simulation. These applications establish relevance, not automatic eligibility as a biomedical ontology.

## Required future package

- A domain-specific use case with a public worked example.
- A statement of which concepts remain in UOGTO and which belong in an applied extension pack.
- Registry-specific scope and governance evidence.
- Current release, DOI, documentation, licence, namespace, and ORCID metadata.
- A human-approved submission decision.

No BioPortal or OBO Foundry submission is made by this track.
