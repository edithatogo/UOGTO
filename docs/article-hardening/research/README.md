# Article-Hardening Research Notes

This directory stores phase-level research outputs for `uogto_article_hardening_protocol_20260624`.

Research roles are declared in `conductor/agents/article-hardening-research-agents.json`.

Research workflow:

1. Assign minimum and phase-specific researchers.
2. Record research actions in `phase-research-log.jsonl`.
3. Write role-specific research notes.
4. Hand off evidence, unresolved gaps, and claim boundaries to peer/editorial/red-team/devil's-advocate review.

The research log is append-only. Add superseding entries when research findings change.
