# RI-HERO Meta-Program

RI-HERO is the meta-program that frames UOGTO work through a health economics research on outcomes lens.

## Purpose
RI-HERO provides a higher-level program structure for:
- governance and operating model design
- ontology implementation and validation
- publication and registry follow-through
- monitoring and maintenance
- evidence synthesis and outcomes mapping
