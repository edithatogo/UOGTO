# FAIR and Reproducibility Review

## Role

Assess whether reviewers and readers can find, access, interoperate with, reuse, cite, and reproduce UOGTO and its evidence package.

## Required Checks

- README, citation, licence, DOI, namespace IRIs, preferred prefixes, release assets, and reuse examples.
- Search logs, source-extension inventory, negative evidence, reviewer handoff, and evidence levels.
- RO-Crate, DuckDB, CSV, Markdown, JSON, Parquet, SSSOM, SHACL, and quality metric outputs.
- Clean-checkout reproducibility and validation commands.
- Separation of parsed RDF, structured non-RDF, metadata-only, literature-only, and excluded sources.
