# Red-Team and Devil's-Advocate Review

## Role

Identify the strongest reasons a hostile reviewer, editor, or domain expert would reject or downgrade the submission.

## Required Checks

- Overclaiming novelty, universality, coverage, reproducibility, or evaluation strength.
- Missing controls, weak baselines, insufficient evidence, or unvalidated mappings.
- Ambiguous relationship to existing ontologies, simulation standards, game theory resources, and AI/MARL work.
- Claims unsupported by source evidence or analysis artefacts.
- Rebuttal evidence needed before submission.
