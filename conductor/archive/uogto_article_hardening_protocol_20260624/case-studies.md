# Article Hardening Case-Study Spine

Use these case studies as the fixed review spine for the article-hardening protocol before broadening UOGTO.

## Cases

1. Auction and mechanism design
2. Voting and social choice
3. Security and Stackelberg games
4. Multi-agent reinforcement learning and Markov games
5. Agent-based policy simulation
6. System-dynamics feedback game
7. LLM-agent and tool-use game
8. Executable trace and provenance

## Use

- Map each case to candidate terms, module coverage, and explicit disposition.
- Record where UOGTO should expand, where only external alignment is warranted, and where the case should be deferred or rejected.
- Use the executable trace and provenance case as the bridge between formal modelling and the evidence register.
- Keep the case studies broad enough to surface missing or adjacent concepts before the ontology is expanded further.
