# Manuscript Submission Revision and Venue Strategy

## Overview

Turn the manuscript and presubmission review evidence into a publication plan
that separates arXiv completion, Nature-level revision, and alternative venue
strategy.

## Functional Requirements

- Complete external arXiv upload, rendered-PDF inspection, and identifier recording when available.
- Convert presubmission reviewer findings into a prioritized manuscript revision backlog.
- Reassess claims discipline, novelty framing, limitations, figures, and venue fit.
- Keep AI/agent review disclosures clear and avoid representing internal review as independent peer review.

## Acceptance Criteria

- A decision memo states the target venue path and required pre-submission fixes.
- Manuscript, supplement, figure, and citation checks pass after revisions.
- arXiv identifier and rendered-PDF approval are recorded once the external step is complete.

## Out of Scope

- Submitting to a journal on behalf of the author.
- Fabricating external peer review or publisher acceptance evidence.
